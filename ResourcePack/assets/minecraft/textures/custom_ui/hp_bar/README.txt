Please place your PNG images in this folder.

Required images:
- hp_frame.png (The background frame for the HP bar)
- hp_fill_0.png (0% HP fill)
- hp_fill_10.png (10% HP fill)
- hp_fill_20.png (20% HP fill)
- ...
- hp_fill_100.png (100% HP fill)

Recommended size: 64x16 or similar, depending on your design.
Ensure the "ascent" in default.json matches your image height/alignment needs.
